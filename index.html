F1 Strategy Simulator

Per-driver strategist calls for every 2026 round, one driver at a time, never the whole grid. References
What this model does and does not account for: safety cars are modelled, as a per-lap chance of a full-course caution that shuffles the pit windows. It does not model retirements/DNFs, red flags, human error (driver mistakes, pit-crew errors, mechanical failures), or mid-race weather changes. Predictions are a strategist's pre-race read of the field on pace and tyre degradation. Real races deviate from this for reasons no pre-race model captures.
