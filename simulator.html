How to use
  1. Pick your car. Click any driver row in the grid on the left (the one tagged You is yours).
  2. Set the grid. Drag driver rows up or down to reorder P1 to P22, or hit “Use qualifying default” for today's real order.
  3. Choose the weather. One condition for the whole race (see below).
  4. Run. A genetic algorithm searches for the pit and tyre strategy that gains your car the most places, then plays the best-case replay.
DRY Slick tyres. Soft (fastest, wears quickest), Medium, Hard (slowest, most durable).
DAMP Intermediate tyres only, about +6s per lap vs dry.
WET Full wet tyres only, about +12s per lap vs dry.

    Limitations: This is a strategy toy, not a prediction. It uses real per-car pace calibrated on prior 2026 rounds (leakage-safe, so early-season and Round 1 are weakest) and simulates safety cars (a per-lap caution chance). It ignores driver error and crashes, reliability retirements/DNFs, red flags, fuel-load strategy, mid-race weather changes, car upgrades, and the full difficulty of overtaking in dirty air (modelled only roughly). Win probabilities come from an injected execution-noise term plus the safety-car draw, and reflect the model's assumptions, not real-world odds.
